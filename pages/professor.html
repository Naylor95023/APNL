研究興趣

OUR FOCUS

以靜電紡絲技術為平台，將前瞻高分子（光電高分子與生醫高分子）製備成奈米纖維，研究其奈米尺度型態、光電元件與組織工程上的應用
Based on the eletrospun nanotechonology, we focus on producing the eletrospun nanofibers prepared from various advanced polymers such as conjugated polymers and biomaterials and futher exploring their nanoscale-morphology, and applications on optoeletronic device and tissue engineering.
